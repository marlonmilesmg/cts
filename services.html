Anti-Wear and Corrosion Application

Nordbak Applications for Wear and Corrosion resistant compounds

Combined Technical Services is in partnership with Nordbak for the distribution and application of Nordbak range of products

The types of applications for which the Nordbak range can be utilised are as follows

  • Crusher and Mill Repair Products

    For the backing and reinforcement of metal liner plates and the filling of gaps between liners and support structures and protection of new liners in trunion ends

  • Wear and Abrasion Resistant Compounds

    For all high wear and corrosion areas in the plant process with various applications from water, slurry, aggregate transportation to protection from acids and chemicals

  • Coatings

    A range of coatings for superb resistance in corrosion, water and acids environments. Can be used on concrete and steel as well as a wide variety of other substrates.

  • Grouts

    Grouts are for the reinforcing of repair of all heavy machinery foundations and offer unique advantages in their combination of strength and economy. One hundred percent(100%) solids with no shrinkage

  • Pulleys

    Application of Hi Impact Pulley compound as well as new pulley manufacturing and lagged to SANS 1669 specifications



Line Boring and Bucket Fabrication

We specialize in repair and manufacture of mild steel structures, chutes, liners, and stainless steel pipes. We have all the equipment & expertise to carry out any fabrication work. We also repair and manufacture LHD buckets including line boring.

We carry out non-destructive testing on all our welding to ensure long life and undisputed high quality to all our fabrication products.

Hydraulics

Combined Technical Services undertakes the complete in-house design, manufacture, repair and testing of hydraulic cylinders.

The hydraulic cylinder specialist can directly control the speed and quality of every task. We have a complete workshop with state of the art 5000 PSI hydraulic cylinder test bench to check on hydraulic faulty cylinders giving quality and guarantee repair. Our test bench is able to test both for non-load(pressure test) and load(dead weight testing)
